Framework

The first ten pages are not a “mini-movie.” They are a reliability test: can a stranger orient quickly, track cause-and-effect, and feel controlled intent.

What we measure

  • Clarity of situation + scene purpose
  • Forward motion (something changes)
  • Character entry competence
  • Tone control and consistency
  • Read friction (confusion, drift, density)

What we do not measure

  • Market trends or packaging
  • Personal taste alignment
  • “Is this brilliant?” subjective judgment
  • Full-act structure outcomes

Methodology

  1. 1 Identify the page-one promise (what the script seems to be).
  2. 2 Track every scene’s functional change (what moves).
  3. 3 Flag friction: missing anchors, blurred intent, tonal wobble.
  4. 4 Assign a risk band and dominant bleed.
  5. 5 Output targeted fixes the writer can execute quickly.
